## Business model

- __Use cases__: COMM42 is used solely to access community services.

- __Initial Generation__: 0 token.

- __Issuance by subscription__: 5 COMM42 minted per unique user (growth mechanism).

- __Controlled emission__: `MultiSig` may decide to mint tokens to reward outstanding contributors or fund major community events.
